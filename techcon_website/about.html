History

The TechCon conference was founded in 2012 in Nairobi as a small gathering of 100 engineers and developers focused in technology.

Over the years it has grown into one of the Afica`s largest conference attracting over 10,000 people from 30+ countries.

It has hosted global leaders and has been launched for over 200 startups.

Today TechCon continues to connect developers,engineers and investors to shape the future of technology.

Mission

Community and Growth;We aim to empower the tech community by providing such opturnities for people to network.
